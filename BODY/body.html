About Us
At Gundam.shop, we are passionate fans of the iconic Gundam franchise, captivating audiences since 1979. Our mission is to share the wonder of Gundam with everyone, providing a space for fans at all levels, from beginners to dedicated collectors. Join us as we explore the vast universe of Gundam, where technology, war, and humanity intertwine to create some of the most compelling narratives in anime history.
